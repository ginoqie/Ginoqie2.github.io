Who I am

About Me

I'm Ginoqie — a dedicated Roblox game developer with over 1 year of hands-on experience building systems, mechanics, and experiences inside Roblox Studio.

My core focus is Roblox game development. I build things like FPS systems, parkour, advanced movement, NPC AI, monster AI, GUI/UI, loading screens, and game mechanics that feel good to play.

I also created a Brainrot Template and an NPC system inspired by Forge's NPC system. On the creative side, I handle thumbnails, posters, and VFX — making sure games look and feel polished from start to finish.

I'm a short-term scripter — I take focused, scoped projects and deliver within a maximum of one week. No long commitments, just clean and fast results.
